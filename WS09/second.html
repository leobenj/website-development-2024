DPS

Melee DPS jobs excel in close combat, requiring proximity to the enemy to maximize damage output. Their gameplay focuses on maintaining "melee uptime" by staying in range during mechanics. Tanks and melee DPS often coordinate to stay close to the boss for efficient damage dealing. Caster DPS jobs combine powerful spells with unique playstyles. They manage cast times and movement while providing utility like mitigation tools and resurrection abilities. Each caster offers distinct strengths, making them versatile contributors to any raid. Physical ranged DPS are highly mobile, maintaining consistent damage regardless of mechanics. While their damage is lower than melee and casters, they excel in utility and offer strong mitigation to handle long-range mechanics.

Samurai

Samurais weild katanas and utilize their knowledge of the traditional Far Eastern swordplay techniques such as laijutsu and the art of "Sen" that includes its three forces Setsu, Getsu and Ka.

Ninja

Ninjas wield daggers to fight foes and are masters of the natural energies that flow through the earth, air and all living creatures. Ninjas channel this power through signs that release devastating assaults upon enemies.

Reaper

Wields a scythe as their weapon and they have the ability to summon a voidsent avatar to guide them through battle.

Monk

Monks draw from the ancient martial arts of the Far East, trained in the secret techniques of unarmed combat. Their power stems from mastering the Fists of Fire, Fists of Wind, and Fists of Earth
