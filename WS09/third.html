Tank

A tank is a robust protector with high health and exceptional defensive capabilities. Their role is to safeguard the team by drawing enemy focus and absorbing the majority of incoming attacks. Currently there are four tank jobs to choose from:

  • Warrior
  • Paladin
  • Dark Knight
  • Gunbreaker
Dark Knight

Wields a greatsword and channels their inner darkness to protect the party.

Paladin

A classic guardian with sword & shield. Uses divine powers to guard allies.

Warrior

Keeps the party safe with pure crushing strength while wielding a greataxe.

Gunbreaker

Wields an aetherically-charged gunblade while safeguarding the party.
